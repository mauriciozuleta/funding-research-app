Welcome to Funding Intelligence Agent

Before You Sign Up — Here’s What I Can Help You With

What I can do for you

Hi! I’m your Funding Intelligence Agent, and here’s how I can support your research journey:

  • I search through public funding APIs, open datasets, and ethically allowed web sources to find opportunities that might fit your work.
  • I read and break down funding calls you paste into the app — highlighting priorities, eligibility clues, constraints, and potential risks.
  • I estimate how well a call aligns with your research profile and explain why.
  • I help you get started with your proposal by drafting sections like the abstract, significance, innovation, or training plan.
  • I can also turn your research profile into a clean, funder-facing banner to share with mentors, collaborators, or potential supporters.
  • And if you want, I’ll send alerts or weekly digests whenever something new appears that might be relevant to you.

How I work (quick and simple)

I combine:

  • Public funding APIs
  • Allowed web scraping
  • Your research profile
  • AI-powered reasoning

to speed up your search and help you prepare early-stage materials.
Think of me as your research grant co-pilot, not a magic grant machine.

What I cannot do

  • I can’t promise that every opportunity I find is complete, up-to-date, or perfect for your situation.
  • I can’t confirm your eligibility — only the official agency can do that.
  • I can’t submit applications, fill out forms, or interact with external portals.
  • I can’t replace your mentors, grant office, or the official guidelines.
  • And because I’m AI, I may occasionally misunderstand something — so anything I generate should always be double-checked by you.

What you’ll still need to do

  • Read each funding call carefully
  • Verify your eligibility
  • Edit and refine all draft text
  • Prepare budgets, letters, and compliance materials
  • Coordinate with your grant office or mentors

I’ll guide you — but you drive the process.

Disclaimer
I provide informational support and AI-generated drafts to help you work more efficiently.
I do not guarantee accuracy, completeness, or success in any funding process.
All final decisions, eligibility checks, application materials, and reliance on information are your responsibility.
Always refer to the official funding announcement and agency guidelines.
